Financial Systems Analyst sought by National Merchant Center, Inc. in Irvine, CA.

 

Duties: Provide quantitative and qualitative analysis services concerning financial information vital to the decision-making process for company and clients.  Collect and prepare technical report data (market/financial analysis) to provide company with optimal financial systems solutions.  Utilize quantitative research results to draw charts and graphs that illustrate financial systems data to merchants.  Apply advanced analytical and quantitative knowledge and skills to develop and maintain comprehensive reporting systems and monitor performance in critical financial systems areas.  Work on customized projects for profit improvement, cost reduction, and systems enhancement.  Conduct data analytics and quantitative research to develop data quality and financial transactions systems solutions. Analyze company’s financial needs, define core issues and customize solutions to company specific requirements.  Oversee company’s end-to-end accumulation and consolidation of financial systems data. Recommend changes and modifications to financial systems.  Review and analyze assets, profitability, and short-term and long-term capabilities of merchants to determine viability of financial systems.  Research, analyze, and evaluate current industry technology and optimal industry practices.  Maintain extensive liaison with financial institutions, corporate partners, credit card processing companies, and government agencies.  Monitor economic markets, credit markets, and international business by analyzing information from financial publications and services, applicable government agencies, trade publications, and other sources. 

 

Requirements: Master’s degree or equivalent in Business Administration, Finance, Economics, or closely related field, plus one (1) year of financial analysis, business management, or related experience analyzing complex financial systems; conducting qualitative and quantitative financial analysis; advising on profit improvement, cost reduction, and financial system viability; researching and evaluating regulatory changes that may affect investments; and performing due diligence research to assess the financial health and stability of companies.

 

Send resume to: HR, National Merchant Center, Inc., 18300 Von Karman Ave., Ste. 850, Irvine, CA 92612, fax (949) 861-3454 or email to jobs@nationalmerchant.com.
